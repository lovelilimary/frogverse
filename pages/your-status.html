How It Works

  • XP & Health System
    Earn XP from renting frogs (1 USD = 1 XP) and daily feeding
  • Rent XP:
    Receive XP immediately when renting NFT (= rental price)
  • Feed XP:
    Receive XP each time you feed (tier dependent: Princess=10, Lady=20, Queen=30, King=50)
  • Level Bonus
    Higher levels receive additional % bonus on daily feeding rewards
  • Retention
    Higher levels reduce % retention when claiming (Level 1: 10% → Level 10: 1%)

How to Earn XP Fast

  • Rent more frogs (King gives most XP when renting)
  • Feed consistently every day (each feed = XP)
  • Maintain high streak (unlock bonuses)
  • King tier: 50 XP/feed, Queen: 30 XP/feed
